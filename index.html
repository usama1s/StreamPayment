Frequently asked questions

StreamPayment is a secure digital platform operated by Accounts General Recoveries Inc., a licensed collection agency. We help customers achieve their financial goals by offering them 24/7 access to convenient payment plans, customized bill repayment schedules and accessible documentation. Secure Payment. On Your Terms.

Many companies partner with us to assist in their debt recovery efforts. This can occur at various stages of delinquency, but you should contact us to discuss the matter and work out an arrangement to address the account. Alternatively you can view account details and options on our payment portal here

We are a collection agency licensed in all provinces and territories we are collecting in. We try to be different from other agencies by focusing on you, our customer. We hope to offer you an experience that helps resolve your account easily with our communication tools and payment options to make this process as stress-free as possible.

If our client has listed your debt on the credit bureaus or has asked us to do the same then paying off the account will result in the record being updated to show that the account is now paid. This, along with other factors, can help to improve your credit score over time.

If you are still getting messages from us it means that our records still show an outstanding balance. Sometimes this can occur when our client has not updated your file in real time. Simply respond to any of our messages to let us know and we will confirm and update our records.

Please let us know about your circumstances and provide your trustee details. We will update our records and deal directly with the trustee who is assisting you.

Due to privacy concerns we need to verify that we are discussing the matter with the right party before proceeding with account details.

We are licensed in all provinces and territories we collect in. Still have more questions? Contact us!
